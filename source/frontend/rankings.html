Top 50 Emission Nations

Our data represent the highest nation's Carbon Dioxide emissions in the world.
Each nation's population is gathered from 2023, and it is used to calculate the aggregate
quotient of its contribution towards climate change.
The data is sorted by this derived quotient, which represents
how much CO2 (in kilograms) is produced per person on average from this relative area.
